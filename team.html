Andrew Stirling

Employment Law Expert

Andrew is an employment law expert. After graduating from the University of Queensland with a Bachelor of Laws and a Bachelor of Economics, Andrew joined the employment law team at Allens, a leading international law firm.

Over the next 12 years, Andrew practiced all aspects of employment law. He was a trusted advisor to many employers, including on their pay obligations, the use of contractual set-off clauses, award and enterprise agreement interpretation, individual flexibility arrangements, and annualised salaries.

Andrew led the Allens employment team in Brisbane for almost five years before joining forces with Tanda to form Tanda PaySure.
